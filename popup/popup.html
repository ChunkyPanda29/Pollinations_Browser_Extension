Quick Guide 📖

1. Context & Right-Click

Highlight text or right-click any image on a website and select "Send to Pollinations" to instantly use it as context for your next generation.

2. Media Hashes (I2I / I2V)

Generations are stored securely returning a unique "hash". You can copy a hash from your History and paste it into a prompt to reuse that exact image as a reference!

3. History Retention

Generations are saved locally. Go to Settings (⚙️) to change how long history is kept. Expired items are securely scrubbed from the Pollinations media server.

4. Pro Tips

• 💎 Models require paid pollen.
• Use the Scale dropdown to generate up to 4K images effortlessly.
